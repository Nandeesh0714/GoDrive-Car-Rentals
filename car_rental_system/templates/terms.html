Car Rental Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility

To rent a car, the driver must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid driver's license.

2. Rental Period

The rental period begins at the time of pickup and ends at the agreed return time. Late returns may incur additional charges.

3. Payment

Payment must be made in full at the start of the rental period. Accepted payment methods include Cash and UPI.

4. Insurance

All rentals include basic insurance coverage. Additional insurance options are available at extra cost.

5. Responsibilities

The renter is responsible for any damages to the vehicle, traffic violations, or loss of property during the rental period.

6. Fuel Policy

The vehicle must be returned with the same level of fuel as at the time of pickup. Additional fees may apply for refueling.

7. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are strictly prohibited and cannot be accommodated under any circumstances, regardless of the timing or reason.

8. Vehicle Condition

The renter must inspect the vehicle before use and report any existing damage. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as rented.

9. Additional Drivers

Additional drivers must be registered and meet the eligibility criteria. Unregistered drivers may void the rental agreement.

10. Mileage Limits

Rentals are subject to daily mileage limits as specified in the rental agreement. Excess mileage charges may apply.

11. Prohibited Uses

The vehicle must not be used for illegal activities, racing, or off-road driving. Violations may result in penalties.

12. Emergency Assistance

In case of breakdown or emergency, renters must contact the rental service immediately for assistance and instructions.
